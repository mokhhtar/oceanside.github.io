Haircut Prices in Oceanside (2025 Guide)

Everything you need to know about salon costs in North County

Check Prices

Finding the right stylist in Oceanside, CA isn't just about the cut; it's often about finding a place that fits your budget without compromising on quality. Whether you are looking for a quick trim near the Pier or a complete transformation in a luxury salon, haircut prices in Oceanside can vary significantly.

In 2025, prices have adjusted across North County due to inflation and rising product costs. This guide breaks down exactly what you can expect to pay for men’s, women’s, and kids' haircuts, so you can book your next appointment with confidence.

Quick answer: Haircut prices in Oceanside range from $25 for kids cuts to $150+ for specialty women's styling, depending on hair length, salon level, and services included.

Average Haircut Prices in Oceanside

Generally, you can expect a wide range of pricing depending on the salon's location (beachfront vs. inland) and the stylist's experience level. On average, a standard haircut in Oceanside ranges from $35 to $120+.

While some chain salons offer budget cuts, independent boutiques and specialized salons—like Oceanside Hair Salon—focus on precision, hair health, and a more personalized experience.

Women’s Haircut Prices in Oceanside

For women, a haircut is rarely just a "cut." It usually involves a consultation, a wash with premium shampoos, conditioning, the cut itself, and a professional blowout.

  • Trim / Maintenance Cut: $55 – $85
  • Transformation Cut (New Style): $90 – $150+
  • Curly Hair Specialist Cut: $100 – $180

Men’s Haircut & Grooming Prices

Men's grooming in Oceanside has evolved from simple buzz cuts to intricate fades and beard styling.

  • Standard Scissor Cut: $35 – $60
  • Skin Fade / Taper: $40 – $70
  • Buzz Cut: $25 – $40
  • Beard Trim & Line Up: $20 – $35

Kids Haircut Prices in Oceanside

Pricing for children (usually under 10 or 12 years old) is typically lower than adult pricing, though this depends on the child's hair thickness and cooperation.

  • Kids Cut (Dry): $25 – $40
  • Kids Cut (Wash & Style): $45 – $65

Note: Some high-end salons charge full price for children if the haircut requires the same amount of time as an adult cut.

Additional Styling & Blowout Costs

Sometimes you don't need a cut, just a wash and style for an event or a night out in downtown Oceanside.

  • Wash & Blowout: $45 – $75
  • Thermal Styling (Flat Iron/Curls): +$15 – $25 add-on
  • Updos (Prom/Wedding): $85 – $150+

Why Do Haircut Prices Vary?

You might wonder why one salon charges $30 and another charges $100. Here are the main factors:

  1. Stylist Level: A Junior Stylist charges less than a Master Stylist with 10+ years of experience.
  2. Education: Stylists who constantly attend workshops to learn the latest trends (shags, wolf cuts, balayage) charge premium rates.
  3. Products Used: Salons using eco-friendly or luxury brands (like Kevin Murphy or Oribe) factor this into the price.
  4. Time: A 15-minute dry cut costs far less than a 60-minute detail-oriented session.
