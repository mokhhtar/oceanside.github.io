Hair Coloring & Balayage

Expert Balayage, Highlights & Color Correction in Oceanside.


The Art of Color in Oceanside

Hair color is not one-size-fits-all. It's an art form that requires a deep understanding of chemistry, skin tone, and lifestyle. Being in Oceanside, we specialize in "lived-in" color techniques that mimic the natural effects of the sun, giving you that effortless California glow.

Specialized Techniques

Balayage: Our most requested service. We hand-paint highlights onto the hair to create a soft, natural gradation of lightness towards the ends. This technique allows for longer time between appointments as there is no harsh root line.

Color Correction: Did a home dye job go wrong? Or did another salon leave you with brassy orange tones? Our master colorists are experts in color correction. We safely remove unwanted pigments and restore your hair to the desired shade while maintaining its integrity.

Protecting Your Color

The Oceanside sun and ocean salt can fade hair color quickly. That's why we use premium color lines enriched with UV filters and conditioning agents. We also provide a customized maintenance plan, recommending the right color-depositing shampoos to keep your tone fresh between visits.
