Our Grooming Services

  • The Skin Fade

    The cleanest look for the coast. We specialize in low, mid, and high skin fades that transition seamlessly from skin to hair without any visible lines. We use foil shavers for the closest finish and detail the hairline for a sharp, modern silhouette.

  • Classic Scissor Cuts

    For gentlemen who prefer longer, textured styles (like the surfer curtain bang or the pompadour). We use point-cutting and texturizing shears to add movement and remove weight without the "freshly cut" stiffness. Perfect for wavy or thick hair.

  • Beard Sculpting & Trims

    A beard is makeup for men. We don't just shorten it; we sculpt it. We enhance your jawline, balance your face shape, and clean up the cheek and necklines. Includes a conditioning beard oil application.

  • Gray Blending (Camo Color)

    Not ready to go fully gray? We offer discreet "camo" color services that blend gray hair away in 5-10 minutes. It leaves a natural, salt-and-pepper look that fades gradually without leaving harsh root lines.

  • The Hot Towel Treatment

    Upgrade any service with our signature hot towel treatment. It opens the pores, softens the beard, and relaxes the mind. It’s the ultimate stress reliever after a long week.

Pro Tips for Men

  • Scalp Health: If you use styling products daily, use a clarifying shampoo once a week to prevent buildup and dandruff.
  • Beard Care: Beard hair is coarser than head hair. Use a dedicated beard oil daily to soften the hair and moisturize the skin underneath to prevent itchiness.
  • Product Choice: For a natural look, choose "Matte Paste" or "Clay." For a sleek, vintage look, choose "Pomade" with shine.
