Bridal & Special Occasions

Your dream wedding hair starts here. On-site and in-salon services.


Your Dream Wedding Hair

Oceanside is a premier wedding destination, with its beautiful beaches and resorts. If you are getting married here, you need a style that holds up against the ocean breeze and lasts from the first photo to the last dance. Our bridal team specializes in long-lasting, photogenic styling.

Services for the Bride & Party

We offer on-site styling (we come to your venue) or in-salon parties. Our services include:

  • Bridal Trials: We recommend a trial run 4-6 weeks before the wedding. We test different looks to find the perfect match for your dress and veil.
  • Updos & Chignons: Classic, elegant styles that keep hair off your neck and secure against the wind.
  • Boho Waves & Braids: Soft, romantic styles that fit the relaxed beach wedding vibe perfectly.
  • Bridesmaids & Flower Girls: Cohesive looks for your entire bridal party.

We recommend booking your wedding date at least 3-6 months in advance as our weekends fill up quickly during the wedding season.

The "Oceanside Finish" Blowout

No haircut is complete without a professional finish. Our signature blowout includes the use of humidity-resistant products that seal the cuticle and provide shine that lasts for days. Whether you want volume, stick-straight sleekness, or bouncy curls, you'll leave our salon feeling runway-ready.

Why Choose Us?

Our team undergoes continuous training to stay updated with the latest trends from Los Angeles and New York, bringing high-end styling to our local Oceanside community. We use only premium, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners during your wash to protect your hair's health.
