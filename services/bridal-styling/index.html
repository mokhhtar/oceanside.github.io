Your Dream Wedding Hair

Oceanside is a premier wedding destination, known for its stunning beaches, luxury resorts like the Seabird and Mission Pacific, and breathtaking sunsets. If you are getting married here, you need a hairstyle that not only looks breathtaking in photos but also holds up against the coastal breeze and lasts from the first look to the last dance.

Our specialized bridal team understands the nuances of beach wedding styling. We don't just pin your hair up; we sculpt it to complement your dress, your veil, and your unique personality. We specialize in long-lasting, photogenic styles that withstand humidity and wind, ensuring you look flawless all day long.

Whether you are a local bride or planning a destination wedding in Oceanside, we offer a stress-free, luxurious experience to kick off your big day.

Services for the Bride & Party

  • The Bridal Trial Run

    We strongly recommend a trial run 4-6 weeks before the wedding. This 90-minute session is crucial. We test different looks, adjust hair accessories, and find the perfect match for your veil. It gives us a chance to get to know your hair's texture and ensures there are no surprises on the big day.

  • Classic Updos & Chignons

    Timeless, elegant, and secure. A classic low bun or sophisticated chignon keeps hair off your neck and secure against the ocean wind. Perfect for formal or black-tie beach weddings where you want a polished, red-carpet look.

  • Boho Waves & Braids

    Soft, romantic, and perfectly imperfect. These styles fit the relaxed California vibe perfectly. We specialize in voluminous braids, fishtails, and loose "mermaid" waves that look effortless but are structurally sound to last all night. We love incorporating flowers, vines, and clips.

  • Bridesmaids & Flower Girls

    We create cohesive looks for your entire bridal party that complement your style while allowing their individual personalities to shine. We ensure the timing flows smoothly so everyone is ready for photos on schedule.

  • On-Site vs. In-Salon Styling

    We offer flexible options to suit your needs. We can bring our team to your hotel suite or wedding venue for ultimate convenience (travel fees apply), or you can host your bridal party in our salon, complete with mimosas and plenty of space to get ready.
